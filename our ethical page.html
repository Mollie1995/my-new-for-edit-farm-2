Our Commitment to Ethical Livestock

At Nature's Bounty Farm, our animals are raised with respect, compassion, and the highest standards of welfare, ensuring healthy animals and wholesome products.

See Our Practices

Foundations of Our Care

We believe that happy, healthy animals are the heart of sustainable farming. Discover the core principles guiding our livestock management.

Pasture-Raised & Free-Range

Our animals enjoy spacious pastures with ample room to roam, graze naturally, and express their natural behaviors. Fresh air and sunshine are always abundant.

Natural, Non-GMO Diet

They are fed a wholesome diet of locally sourced, non-GMO grains and forages, free from artificial additives, hormones, or unnecessary antibiotics.

Stress-Free Environment

We maintain peaceful and calm environments, minimizing stress through gentle handling, quiet surroundings, and consistent care routines.

Proactive Health Management

Preventative care, clean living conditions, and natural remedies are prioritized to ensure the robust health and well-being of every animal on our farm.

Clean Water & Shelter

Constant access to fresh, clean water and protective, well-ventilated shelters are fundamental to their comfort and health, year-round.

Small Herd Sizes

By keeping our herd sizes manageable, we ensure individualized attention and allow for stronger natural social dynamics among the animals.
